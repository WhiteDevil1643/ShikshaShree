Frequently Asked Questions

What is the address of ShikshaShree Classes?

Our institute is located at Nehru Yuva Kendra, Bhagat Singh Chawk, Haridwar, UK. We are easily accessible and situated in a student-friendly environment.

What are the course fees?

Our course fees vary based on the student's class level. We offer affordable, value-packed plans designed for Classes 1 to 10. For more information, visit Courses.

Can students select specific subjects or full packages?

Yes, students have the flexibility to choose between individual subjects or full subject packages. We offer customized options to suit each student's learning needs and goals.

What are the class timings?

Classes are scheduled in flexible time slots, including after-school and weekend batches. Timings vary based on the class and selected course. You can contact us for the current timetable.

Are there demo classes available before enrollment?

Yes, we offer free demo classes so that students and parents can experience our teaching style and environment before enrolling.

What is the mode of teaching — online, offline, or both?

We currently offer offline classes at our center with personal attention and live interaction. However, based on demand, we may also provide online support or hybrid options in the future.
